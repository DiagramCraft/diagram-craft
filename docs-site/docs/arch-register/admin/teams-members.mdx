# Teams And Members

Teams and members are related, but they are not the same administrative concern.

## Teams

Teams define ownership boundaries for entities and projects.

Use teams to:

- assign responsibility
- control team-scoped access
- keep ownership aligned with the people doing the work

## Members

Members are the users who belong to the workspace. Member management is where you assign workspace roles.

  alt="Members screen in Arch Register"
  sources={{

  }}

## Why They Are Separate

Keep these mental models distinct:

- a person can be a workspace member without owning content
- a team can own content without replacing workspace roles
- team-role assignment and workspace-role assignment solve different access problems
