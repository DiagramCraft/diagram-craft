## Permission Layers

The main layers are:

- global permissions
- workspace roles
- team roles

## Workspace Roles

Workspace roles govern capabilities across the workspace shell and settings screens.

Typical concerns include:

- access to settings
- member and team administration
- schema editing
- audit visibility

## Team Roles

Team roles govern access to content owned by a team. They matter most for entities and projects where ownership is the
main access boundary.

## Global Permissions

Global permissions sit outside one workspace and cover platform-wide administration such as global role assignment.
